# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project presents a **Sales Performance Dashboard** built using Power BI.
It analyzes sales data to uncover key business insights such as revenue trends, top-performing products, and sales representatives.

---

##  Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing products and sales reps
* Track monthly revenue trends
* Compare revenue across regions

---

##  Dataset

The dataset contains **1500 sales records** with the following fields:

* Order ID
* Order Date
* Region
* Product
* Category
* Sales Representative
* Orders
* Revenue

---

##  Key Metrics (KPIs)

* **Total Revenue:** 152M
* **Total Orders:** 6723
* **Total Transactions:** 1500

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Revenue by Region

* Compares revenue across regions
* Helps identify the best-performing region

### 2️⃣ Revenue by Product

* Shows product-wise revenue contribution
* Identifies top-selling products

### 3️⃣ Top 3 Products

* Highlights highest revenue-generating products

### 4️⃣ Revenue by Sales Representative

* Compares performance of sales reps
* Identifies top contributors

### 5️⃣ Monthly Revenue Trend

* Displays revenue trend over time
* Helps identify growth and decline patterns

---

##  Tools Used

* Power BI (Data Visualization)
* SQL (Data Analysis)
* Excel (Data Preparation)

---

##  Insights

* Laptop is the highest revenue-generating product
* West region shows strong performance
* Top sales reps contribute significantly to total revenue
* Revenue shows fluctuations across months
